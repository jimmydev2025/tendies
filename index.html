What is Tendies?

Tendies is a cryptocurrency inspired by the internet meme culture, featured on 4 chan January 29th, 2015 with the #4tendies movement and threads symbolizing the love for chicken tenders (or "tendies" as they're affectionately called). It's a coin that embodies humor, community spirit, and the joy of simple pleasures, like a plate full of crispy, golden tendies.

Based Tendies $TENDIES

First introduced On January 29th, 2015, when the subreddit /r/4chan was renamed #4tendies in reference to the numerous tendies threads and images being uploaded to the site.Tendies has become part of broader meme culture symbolizing financial gain or profits as seen by its meaning within the r/WallStreetBets community.
